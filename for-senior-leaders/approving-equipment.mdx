## What you've been sent

You've most likely been given a **PDF or ZIP file** exported from Usafety. Each PDF inside covers one employee and contains:

- Their name, department and assessment date
- The compliance status (compliant / non-compliant / not assessed)
- A list of recommended equipment with model, supplier, unit cost and quantity
- Any assessor notes or photos

## What you're being asked to decide

For each recommendation, you're approving one of three outcomes:

1. **Approve and order** — the H&S manager can place the order
2. **Approve with changes** — e.g. a different supplier, different model, different quantity
3. **Reject** — with a reason (cost, alternative accommodation, existing equipment)
