When you sign into Usafety, the first page you see is the **organisation dashboard**. This is a short guide on how to read it if your job is to approve spend or review compliance once a month, not to live in the tool day-to-day.

## What's on the page

The dashboard shows five stat cards across the top, plus three feature areas below.

### The five stat cards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="This Week" icon="calendar-day">
    How many assessments are scheduled or completed in the current calendar week. The pulse check — is this week busy or quiet?
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Total Assessments" icon="clipboard-list">
    The cumulative count of every assessment your organisation has ever run. Useful for trend context rather than operational decisions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Completed" icon="circle-check">
    How many assessments have finished and are ready to review. This is the number your H&S manager is working through each month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Upcoming" icon="clock">
    Assessments scheduled for the future — ones employees have booked but haven't attended yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Needs Attention" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    The single most important number for SLT. This is the count of things the H&S team hasn't actioned — non-compliant rows, overdue follow-ups, stuck fulfilment items. If it's climbing month-on-month, that's a conversation to have.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Feature areas

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Book Remote Assessments" icon="link">
    A shortcut card to get started if you don't have an active booking link. It jumps to the Booking Links page so you can create one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Recent Booking Links" icon="list">
    The last handful of booking links your team has created, with their status (*Pending*, *Approved*, *Rejected*, *Expired*). Useful for SLT to see what's actively accepting bookings right now.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Activity Timeline" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    A running feed of what's happened recently — new bookings, completed assessments, equipment status changes, comments added. Think of this as the "what's new" feed you'd scan once a week.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What SLT usually cares about

If you're reviewing monthly, three things matter most:

1. **Is *Needs Attention* climbing or shrinking?** Month-over-month movement in this number tells you whether the H&S team is keeping up or falling behind.
2. **What equipment are we about to sign off on?** This isn't on the overview dashboard — open **Reports → Equipment Compliance** and filter to *Non-compliant + To Do*. That's the queue for SLT to approve.
3. **Are we meeting our annual reassessment obligation?** On **Reports → Equipment Compliance**, sort by *Assessment Date*. Anyone older than 12 months is overdue.

## The numbers to ask your H&S manager

Whether or not you want to learn the tool yourself, these are the four numbers worth asking about in a monthly review:

- **% of staff assessed in the last 12 months** (target: 100%)
- **% of staff currently compliant** (target: improving trend)
- **Equipment items in *To Do* status** (your SLT-approval queue)
- **Equipment items stuck in *Ordered* for more than 4 weeks** (supplier chase list)
