<Note>
  This page is being written for senior leaders. It'll cover: what each status colour means, how to interpret compliance percentages, and which numbers are worth watching.

  For now, the practical version: on the compliance dashboard, **non-compliant** means an employee has outstanding equipment needs, **compliant** means they're fully set up, and **not assessed** means they've never completed an assessment. Most H&S conversations focus on moving the non-compliant number down and the not-assessed number to zero.
</Note>
