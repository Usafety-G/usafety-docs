---
title: "Tracking equipment recommendations"
description: "How equipment recommendations flow from a DSE assessment into the compliance dashboard."
---

When an assessment flags that an employee needs equipment — a new chair, a footrest, a second monitor — that recommendation becomes a trackable item on the Equipment Compliance dashboard with its own fulfilment lifecycle.

## Where recommendations come from

A recommendation is created automatically by the assessor during a DSE assessment. They pick items from your [equipment catalogue](/equipment-and-compliance/equipment-catalogue) and flag them against the employee's record. The recommendation then appears on the Equipment Compliance dashboard in its default state.

## The fulfilment lifecycle

Every recommendation moves through eight possible states:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Recommended">
    Created by the assessment. Default state — nothing has been decided yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approved">
    You or SLT have signed off that it should be bought.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ordered">
    The purchase order has been placed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delivered">
    The equipment has arrived at your site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Installed">
    The equipment is set up at the employee's workstation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed">
    The employee is using it. Terminal state — compliance is satisfied for this item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Two additional non-terminal states exist: **Rejected** (SLT or the employee declined) and **Returned** (the item was sent back — wrong spec, damaged, no longer needed). Both are kept in the record for audit but don't count against compliance.

## Updating a recommendation

From **Reports → Equipment Compliance**, click the Quick View button on the employee row. The *Equipment Required* section lists every recommendation with its current status — change the dropdown next to any item to move it to a new state.

<Tip>
  Marking every item for an employee as **Completed** is what flips their overall compliance status from non-compliant to compliant.
</Tip>

## Notes and comments

Each assessment has a comment thread attached to it (not to individual equipment items). Use it to record context that applies to the whole assessment:

- Purchase order numbers
- Delivery dates
- Conversations with suppliers
- Why a recommendation was declined

Open the comment thread from the assessment Quick View — click the menu button and choose **Add Comment**.
