---
title: "Tracking equipment recommendations"
description: "How equipment recommendations flow from a DSE assessment into the compliance dashboard."
---

When an assessment flags that an employee needs equipment — a new chair, a footrest, a second monitor — that recommendation doesn't just sit in the report. It becomes a trackable item in the **Equipment Compliance** dashboard, with its own fulfilment status.

## Where recommendations come from

A recommendation is created automatically when:

- **The self-assessment flags an issue.** If an employee answers that their chair doesn't support their lower back, Usafety maps that answer to a "chair" recommendation.
- **The assessor adds one during an in-person review.** Certified assessors can add bespoke recommendations with photos and notes during a visit.
- **An admin adds one manually.** From the employee profile, click **Equipment → Add recommendation** to create one by hand (useful for one-off accommodations).

## The fulfilment lifecycle

Every recommendation moves through a simple lifecycle:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pending">
    Created by the assessment. Nothing's been ordered yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approved">
    You (or SLT) have signed off that it should be bought. Use this when you've got budget approval but haven't placed the order yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ordered">
    The equipment is on its way.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Installed">
    The equipment has been delivered and set up at the employee's workstation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed">
    The employee has been told about the new equipment and is using it. This is the terminal state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also mark a recommendation **Rejected** if SLT declines it or the employee turns it down.

## Updating a recommendation

From **Reports → Equipment Compliance**, click any employee row to open the Quick View. The Equipment tab shows every recommendation with its current status. Click the status dropdown to change it, or click **Track fulfilment** to update multiple items at once.

<Tip>
  Marking an item as **Completed** also updates the employee's overall compliance status, which is what the compliance dashboard counts.
</Tip>

## Adding notes and comments

Every recommendation has a comments thread. Use it to record:

- Purchase order numbers
- Delivery dates
- Conversations with suppliers
- Why a recommendation was declined

Comments show up on the exported PDF reports, so SLT see your audit trail when you [export equipment reports](/equipment-and-compliance/exporting-equipment-reports).
