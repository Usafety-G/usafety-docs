---
title: "The equipment catalogue"
description: "Set up your organisation's master list of approved equipment."
---

The equipment catalogue is your organisation's master list of chairs, monitors, monitor arms, footrests, keyboards and anything else a DSE assessment might recommend. When an assessment generates a recommendation, it picks from this catalogue — so getting the catalogue right at the start makes everything downstream cleaner.

## Opening the catalogue

Go to **Settings → Equipment**. You'll see every piece of equipment your organisation has added.

## Adding an item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add equipment">
    Top-right of the catalogue page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    At minimum: **name**, **category** (chair, monitor, etc.) and **manufacturer**. Optional but recommended: **model**, **SKU**, **supplier**, **unit cost** and **currency**. Adding cost is what unlocks the budget-rollup features for SLT reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The item is immediately available for assessors and admins to recommend.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing and deleting

Click any row to edit. Deleting is only allowed for items that haven't been recommended to an employee yet — if an item has history, you can archive it instead, which hides it from the picker but keeps the audit trail intact.

## Why the catalogue matters

- **Consistency.** Assessors pick from the same list every time, so "ergonomic chair" means the same thing across every report.
- **Budget visibility.** Unit costs roll up into the compliance dashboard and SLT reports.
- **Faster fulfilment.** SKU and supplier fields mean whoever is placing the order has everything they need without chasing down detail.
