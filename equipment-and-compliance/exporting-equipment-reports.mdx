---
title: "Exporting equipment reports"
description: "Download PDF or ZIP reports of employee equipment recommendations, filtered by date, team or status — ideal for SLT approval."
---

If you need to bring equipment recommendations to your senior leadership team (SLT) for approval, Usafety can export full PDF reports for one employee, a handful, or everyone in your organisation. You can filter by department, date range, assessment type and compliance status before you export, so SLT only see the rows that matter.

<Info>
  Every exported PDF contains the full breakdown SLT usually ask for: employee details, assessment date, compliance status, equipment required (with model, supplier and cost), quantities and any assessor notes.
</Info>

## Open the Equipment Compliance report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Reports → Equipment Compliance">
    In the left sidebar, click **Reports**, then **Equipment Compliance**. You'll see a table of every employee with an assessment and their current equipment-compliance status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filter down to what you need

Use the toolbar above the table to narrow the data:

| Filter | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Search** | Free-text search by employee name |
| **Department** | Limit to a specific team or site |
| **Equipment type** | Only show rows that need a certain category (e.g. chairs, monitor arms, footrests) |
| **Compliance status** | Compliant / non-compliant / not assessed |
| **Remediation status** | To do / in progress / done |
| **Assessment date** | Date range for when the assessment was completed |

<Tip>
  Filters are stored in the URL, so once you've set them up the way SLT wants, you can bookmark or share the link with another admin.
</Tip>

## Select the employees you want

You have three ways to select rows:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="One employee">
    Click the row to open the Quick View panel, then click **Download Report**. A single PDF downloads to your computer.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="A few employees">
    Tick the checkbox on the left of each row. A bulk-action bar appears at the bottom of the page.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Everyone (across all pages)">
    Tick the **header checkbox** at the top of the first column. Usafety will ask whether you want to select just the current page or **all rows that match your filters across every page** — click the "select all across all pages" option.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Download

Once you have rows selected, open the action bar at the bottom of the page and click **Download**.

- **One employee selected** → you get a single PDF named after the employee.
- **Multiple employees selected** → Usafety bundles every employee's PDF into a ZIP file named `DSE_Reports_YYYY-MM-DD.zip`. Each PDF inside is named after the employee.

<Warning>
  Bulk downloads process each report one at a time on the server, so a ZIP of 50+ employees can take a minute or two. Leave the tab open until the download starts — you'll see progress toasts in the bottom right.
</Warning>

## What's in each PDF

Every PDF contains:

- Employee name, email and department
- Assessment date and assessor (if in-person)
- Overall compliance status (compliant / non-compliant / not assessed)
- Every piece of equipment recommended, with:
  - Name, model, description
  - Supplier and unit cost (if set in your equipment catalogue)
  - Quantity needed
  - Current fulfilment status (pending, ordered, installed, completed)
  - Any assessor notes
- Remediation status and any comments added by your team

## Typical workflows

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Monthly SLT equipment approval" icon="calendar-days">
    1. Filter by **Assessment date** → last month.
    2. Filter by **Compliance status** → *non-compliant* (people who still need equipment).
    3. Select all across all pages.
    4. Download as ZIP.
    5. Attach the ZIP to your SLT board pack.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Departmental budget request" icon="building">
    1. Filter by **Department** → the team you're requesting budget for.
    2. Filter by **Remediation status** → *to do*.
    3. Select all, download ZIP.
    4. The cost fields in each PDF let finance tally the total spend.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Single-employee sign-off" icon="user">
    If SLT only need sign-off on one person (e.g. a bespoke chair for an accommodation request), open the employee row, click **Download Report**, and email the PDF.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Can I export to Excel or CSV instead of PDF?

Not yet — the current export is PDF per employee, bundled into a ZIP for bulk. Most organisations bring the PDFs to SLT directly because they include photos and assessor notes, which spreadsheets can't represent.

If you specifically need a flat spreadsheet of equipment + cost + employee for finance, email [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) — we can generate a one-off CSV from the backend while we work on building an in-dashboard version.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Download button is greyed out">
    You need at least one row selected. Tick a checkbox or the header checkbox first.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A PDF says 'No report available'">
    That employee doesn't have a completed assessment yet. Share a [booking link](/bookings-and-scheduling/booking-links) with them so they can book a slot.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The ZIP download failed halfway">
    Usafety processes reports one at a time and continues even if an individual report fails. If the whole ZIP fails, try selecting a smaller batch (50 at a time is a safe ceiling). If it keeps failing, contact [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I need older data — the date filter stops at the assessment date">
    The date filter is based on when the assessment was completed, not when the employee joined. If you need historical data going back further than Usafety currently shows, contact support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
