The Equipment Compliance dashboard (**Reports → Equipment Compliance**) is the single page most H&S managers live in. It shows every employee, their assessment status, and where they are in the equipment fulfilment process.

## The columns explained

| Column | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Employee** | Name, email and avatar |
| **Department** | Pulled from the employee record |
| **Assessment date** | When the most recent assessment was completed |
| **Equipment needed** | List of pending equipment items, if any |
| **Compliance status** | *Compliant*, *Non-compliant* or *Not assessed* — the rolled-up state |
| **Remediation status** | Your team's workflow state: *No action needed*, *To do*, *In progress*, *Done* |

## Compliance status vs remediation status

People get these confused. The short version:

- **Compliance status** is derived from Usafety's data. It's *compliant* when the employee has a recent assessment and no outstanding equipment needs, *non-compliant* when they have outstanding needs, and *not assessed* if they've never completed an assessment.
- **Remediation status** is what your team sets manually. It tracks *your* workflow — whether you've acted on the recommendations yet.

A row can be **non-compliant + done** (you've ordered everything but the employee hasn't confirmed installation), or **compliant + to do** (you still need to follow up with them for some reason).

## Filtering

Every column has a filter. The most useful combinations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do I still need to order this month?">
    Filter by **Compliance status = non-compliant** and **Remediation status = to do**. These are people who need equipment that you haven't started working on yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who's fully sorted?">
    Filter by **Compliance status = compliant**. Use this for exec reports that show what percentage of staff are fully compliant.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who's never done an assessment?">
    Filter by **Compliance status = not assessed**. These are the people to send assessments to next.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's overdue?">
    Filter by **Assessment date = more than 12 months ago**. Usafety recommends annual reassessment.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Cross-page selection

The dashboard paginates at 50 rows. To select everyone who matches your filters (not just the current page), tick the header checkbox — Usafety will ask whether you want to select the current page or **all X rows across all pages**. Choose the latter when you're exporting for SLT.

See [Exporting equipment reports](/equipment-and-compliance/exporting-equipment-reports) for the full bulk-export flow.
