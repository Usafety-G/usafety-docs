The Equipment Compliance dashboard (**Reports → Equipment Compliance**) is the single page most H&S managers live in. It shows every employee with an assessment, their compliance status, and your team's remediation workflow state for each one.

## The columns

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Employee** | Name, email and avatar |
| **Compliance** | *Compliant*, *Non-compliant* or *Not assessed* — Usafety's rolled-up data view |
| **Department** | From the employee record |
| **Remediation Status** | Your team's workflow state (inline editable) |
| **Assessment Date** | When the most recent assessment was completed |
| **Follow-up Needed** | Whether this row needs attention |
| **Actions** | Quick View, Download Report, and row-level actions |

## Compliance status vs remediation status

These two often get confused. The short version:

- **Compliance status** is derived from Usafety's data. It's *compliant* when the employee has a recent assessment and no outstanding equipment needs, *non-compliant* when they have outstanding needs, and *not assessed* when they've never completed an assessment.
- **Remediation status** is what your team sets manually. It tracks *your* workflow — whether you've acted on the recommendations yet. Values: *No Action Needed*, *To Do*, *In Progress*, *Done*.

A row can be **non-compliant + Done** (you've ordered everything but the employee hasn't confirmed installation), or **compliant + To Do** (you still need to follow up with them for some reason). The two statuses answer different questions.

## Inline editing

The **Remediation Status** column is editable directly from the table — click the dropdown in any row to change it without opening a side panel. Compliance status is not editable: it's derived from the underlying data.

## Filtering

Every column has a filter. The most useful combinations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do I still need to order this month?">
    Filter by **Compliance = Non-compliant** and **Remediation Status = To Do**. These are people who need equipment that you haven't started working on yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who's fully sorted?">
    Filter by **Compliance = Compliant**. Use this for exec reports that show the percentage of staff fully compliant.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who's never done an assessment?">
    Filter by **Compliance = Not assessed**. These are the people to share a booking link with next.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's overdue?">
    Filter by **Assessment Date = more than 12 months ago**. Usafety recommends annual reassessment.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pagination and cross-page selection

The dashboard paginates at 50 rows by default. To select everyone who matches your filters — not just the current page — tick the header checkbox and choose to select across all pages when prompted. This is how you bulk-export a full filtered set; see [Exporting equipment reports](/equipment-and-compliance/exporting-equipment-reports).
