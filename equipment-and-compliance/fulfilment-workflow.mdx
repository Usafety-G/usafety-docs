---
title: "Fulfilment workflow"
description: "How equipment items move from recommended to installed."
---

Once an assessment recommends a piece of equipment, it becomes a fulfilment item. Fulfilment items have their own lifecycle (pending → approved → ordered → installed → completed) so you can track every piece of equipment across your organisation from one place.

## The states

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pending">
    Default state. The assessment has recommended this item, but nothing has happened yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Approved">
    SLT or finance has signed off. Use this to mark items that are cleared to buy but not yet ordered.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ordered">
    The purchase order is placed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Installed">
    The equipment is on the employee's desk and set up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Completed">
    The employee has confirmed they're using it. The item stops counting against compliance at this point.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Rejected">
    SLT declined, the employee refused, or the item is no longer needed. Rejected items are kept for audit but don't affect compliance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updating a single item

Open the employee's Quick View from the Equipment Compliance dashboard, go to the Equipment tab, and change the dropdown next to any item.

## Bulk updates

If a whole shipment has arrived, open the employee profile and click **Mark all as installed** — or use the bulk action bar on the Equipment Compliance dashboard to mark multiple employees' equipment at once.

## Comments and audit trail

Every status change is logged with the user and timestamp. You can add a comment at any time (e.g. "PO #12345, expected 10 May"). The comment thread appears on the exported PDF report.
