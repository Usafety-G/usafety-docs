---
title: "Fulfilment workflow"
description: "How equipment items move from recommended through to completed."
---

Once an assessment recommends a piece of equipment for an employee, it becomes a fulfilment item. Fulfilment items have their own lifecycle so you can track every piece of equipment your organisation has committed to buying, in progress, or delivered.

## The states

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Recommended">
    The assessment has recommended this item but nothing has been decided yet. Default state for a new recommendation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Approved">
    SLT or finance has signed off. Use this to mark items that are cleared to buy but not yet ordered.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ordered">
    The purchase order has been placed with the supplier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Delivered">
    The equipment has arrived at your site but hasn't been set up at the employee's workstation yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Installed">
    The equipment is on the employee's desk and set up for use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Completed">
    The employee has confirmed they're using it. The item stops counting against compliance at this point.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Rejected">
    SLT declined, the employee refused, or the item is no longer needed. Rejected items are kept for audit but don't affect compliance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Returned">
    An item that was delivered or installed has been sent back — wrong spec, damaged, or no longer required. Kept for audit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updating an item

From **Reports → Equipment Compliance**, click the Quick View button on any employee row. The *Equipment Required* section in the side panel shows every item with its current status — change the dropdown next to any item to move it to a new state.

<Tip>
  Marking every item for an employee as **Completed** is what flips their overall compliance status from non-compliant to compliant.
</Tip>
