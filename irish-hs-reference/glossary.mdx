---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Key terms used across Usafety and the DSE assessment space."
---

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **DSE** | Display Screen Equipment. Any alphanumeric or graphic display screen, regardless of the display process involved (covers monitors, laptops, tablets used as workstations). |
| **DSE assessment** | A structured review of an employee's workstation, chair, screen, keyboard, posture and working environment. |
| **Self-assessment** | A DSE assessment the employee completes themselves via a questionnaire, without an in-person visit. |
| **In-person assessment** | A DSE assessment conducted by a certified assessor on-site. |
| **Compliance status** | Usafety's rolled-up status for an employee: *compliant*, *non-compliant* or *not assessed*. Derived automatically from the data. |
| **Remediation status** | Your team's workflow state on acting on findings: *no action needed*, *to do*, *in progress*, *done*. Set manually. |
| **Equipment recommendation** | A piece of equipment (chair, monitor arm, footrest) that an assessment has identified the employee needs. |
| **Fulfilment** | The lifecycle of an equipment recommendation from pending → approved → ordered → installed → completed. |
| **Assessor** | A certified individual (often external) who conducts in-person DSE assessments. |
| **SLT** | Senior Leadership Team — the group that approves equipment budgets. |
| **HSA** | Health and Safety Authority, the Irish national body for workplace health and safety. |
| **S.I. No. 299 of 2007** | The Safety, Health and Welfare at Work (General Application) Regulations 2007 — the specific Irish regulations covering DSE work. |
