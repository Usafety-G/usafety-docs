<Note>
  This page is being written. The Health and Safety Authority (HSA) publishes guidance on DSE assessments, including a free self-assessment checklist. Usafety's default assessment template is designed to align with the HSA's guidance.

  The HSA's DSE guidance is available at [hsa.ie](https://www.hsa.ie/).
</Note>
