<Note>
  This page is being written. The Safety, Health and Welfare at Work Act 2005 is the primary piece of Irish health and safety legislation. It imposes general duties on employers to ensure, so far as is reasonably practicable, the safety, health and welfare of their employees — including the workstation and equipment they use.

  The full text is available on the [Irish Statute Book](https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/eli/2005/act/10/).
</Note>
