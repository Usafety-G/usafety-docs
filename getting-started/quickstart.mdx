This guide walks a new organisation admin from signup to their first completed DSE assessment. If you already have staff imported and just need to send an assessment, jump to [Sending an assessment](/dse-assessments/sending-an-assessment).

## Before you start

You'll need:

- An admin invite to your Usafety organisation (check your inbox for an email from Usafety)
- At least one employee's name and email to send an assessment to
- A few minutes

<Note>
  Usafety supports Microsoft 365 / Outlook sign-in. If your organisation uses Microsoft, your admin can enable single sign-on — see [Microsoft 365 sync](/for-administrators/microsoft-365-sync).
</Note>

## 1. Accept your invite

1. Open the invitation email from Usafety and click **Accept invite**.
2. Create a password, or sign in with Microsoft if SSO is enabled.
3. You'll land on your organisation dashboard.

## 2. Add your first employee

You have two options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add one employee">
    1. In the sidebar, click **Staff**.
    2. Click **Add employee** in the top right.
    3. Fill in name, email, department and job title.
    4. Click **Save**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import a CSV">
    1. In the sidebar, click **Staff**.
    2. Click **Import**.
    3. Drop in a CSV with at least `name` and `email` columns.
    4. Map any custom columns to Usafety fields and click **Import**.

    See [Importing staff via CSV](/importing-data/importing-staff) for the full guide.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 3. Send an assessment

1. Open the employee's profile from the **Staff** list.
2. Click **Send assessment**.
3. Choose the assessment type (typically **DSE self-assessment**).
4. Click **Send**. The employee will receive an email with a link to complete the assessment — no login required.

## 4. Review the result

Once the employee submits the assessment, you'll get an email and the record appears on the **Assessments** page.

1. Open **Assessments** in the sidebar.
2. Click the completed row to open the Quick View panel.
3. Review the answers, photos and any equipment recommendations.
4. Set the remediation status to **To do**, **In progress** or **Done** as you act on it.

Full detail: [Reviewing a completed assessment](/dse-assessments/reviewing-assessments).

## 5. Track equipment compliance

If the assessment flagged equipment needs (new chair, monitor arm, footrest, etc.), they'll show up under **Reports → Equipment compliance**.

From there you can filter by department or date, update fulfilment status, and — when it's time to get sign-off — [export a PDF report for SLT approval](/equipment-and-compliance/exporting-equipment-reports).
