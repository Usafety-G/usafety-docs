This guide takes a new organisation admin from accepting their invite to having a live booking link that employees can use to book their own DSE assessment slots. Budget about 10 minutes.

The Usafety workflow most organisations follow is:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept your invite" />
  <Step title="Add other admins to your organisation" />
  <Step title="Create a booking link with the dates you want assessments to happen" />
  <Step title="Wait for Usafety to approve it (usually same-day)" />
  <Step title="Share the link with your employees — they book their own slots" />
</Steps>

<Info>
  Usafety also supports importing or manually adding employees and booking on their behalf. If that's the flow you need, jump to [Importing staff via CSV](/importing-data/importing-staff). Most organisations use the booking-link flow as their primary onboarding.
</Info>

## 1. Accept your invite

When Usafety sets up your organisation, you'll receive an email from `no-reply@usafety.ie` with an **Accept invite** button.

1. Click **Accept invite** in the email.
2. You'll land on the accept-invite page. If you're new, create a password — or sign in with Microsoft 365 if your organisation uses it.
3. Once accepted, you're redirected straight to your organisation dashboard.

## 2. Add other admins

Most organisations want two or three admins on Usafety — the primary H&S manager and one or two backups.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → People">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **People**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Admin">
    The **Invite Admin** button is in the top-right of the People page. A dialog titled *Invite Organization Admin* opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their details">
    Fill in their **email address** (required) and **name** (optional), then send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They accept">
    Your colleague gets an email with an **Accept invite** button. When they click it and finish signing up, they'll appear in the People list as an active admin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Everyone invited from the People page is added as an **admin** with full access to your organisation. Usafety also supports a *member* role with read-only access; if you need that, contact [support@usafety.ie](mailto:support@usafety.ie).
</Note>

## 3. Create a booking link

This is the step that turns Usafety on for your employees. You'll define the dates and times they can book, and Usafety generates a public link you can share.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Booking Links">
    Click **Booking Links** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Link">
    The **Create Link** button is in the top-right. A dialog titled *Create New Booking Link* opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the basics">
    - **Name** — something you'll recognise, e.g. *Dublin HQ — April rollout*
    - **Scheduling pattern** — *Weekly*, *Daily*, *One-time* or *Custom*
    - **Start and end date** — the window employees can book within
    - **Days** and **time slots** — morning (9AM–12:30PM), afternoon (1:30PM–5PM), or both
    - **Booking type** — *Remote* or *In person* (add a location if in-person)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Booking Link">
    The link is saved with **Pending** status. It's not yet live — Usafety needs to approve it first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Full detail on every field: [Booking links](/bookings-and-scheduling/booking-links).

## 4. Wait for approval

Every booking link is reviewed by the Usafety team before it goes live. This is usually same-day during Irish business hours. You'll get an email the moment it's approved, and the status on your Booking Links page will flip from **Pending** to **Approved**.

## 5. Share the link

Once the link is **Approved**, open it from the Booking Links page. You'll see the public URL — it looks like `https://app.usafety.ie/book/<token>`. Click the copy icon next to it.

Then share it however suits your organisation:

- Company-wide email
- Internal Slack or Teams channel
- HR intranet page
- Onboarding pack for new starters

Employees click the link, pick a date and time, fill in a short form, and they're booked. You'll see each new booking appear on your **Assessments** page as it happens.
