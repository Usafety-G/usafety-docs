title: "Inviting your team"
description: "Add H&S managers, ops leads and assessors to your Usafety organisation."
---

Usafety is designed to be shared. Most organisations have a primary H&S manager plus one or two backup admins, and sometimes an external assessor or two.

## Invite a colleague

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open workspace settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Workspace → Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Click **Invite member**. Enter their work email, choose a role (**Admin** or **Member** — see [Roles & permissions](/getting-started/roles-and-permissions)), and click **Send**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They accept">
    Your colleague receives an email with an **Accept invite** button. It takes them to a short signup flow where they set a password or sign in with Microsoft 365.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They show up in the members list">
    Refresh the members page — their status will flip from *Pending* to *Active* as soon as they accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Inviting an assessor is a different flow. Assessors are added from **Admin → Instructors** (if you have access) or by contacting [support@usafety.ie](mailto:support@usafety.ie).
</Tip>

## Resending or cancelling an invite

From **Settings → Workspace → Members**, click the menu next to any pending invite to resend or cancel it. Resent invites use the original link and won't expire the pending invite.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="They didn't get the email">
    Ask them to check their spam folder first — invitation emails come from `no-reply@usafety.ie`. If it's not there, cancel the invite and send a fresh one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The invite link says it's expired">
    Invitation links expire after 14 days. Cancel the old invite in **Settings → Members** and send a new one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="They used a different email when accepting">
    Usafety ties the invite to the email address you entered. If they sign up with a personal email instead, the invite won't bind. Cancel and re-invite with the correct address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
