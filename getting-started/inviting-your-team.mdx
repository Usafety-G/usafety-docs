Most organisations want two or three admins on Usafety — a primary H&S manager plus one or two backups. Everyone you invite from the People page is added as a full organisation admin.

## Invite an admin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → People">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **People**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Admin">
    The **Invite Admin** button is in the top-right. A dialog titled *Invite Organization Admin* opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the form">
    Enter their **email address** (required) and **name** (optional). Click the send button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They accept">
    Your colleague receives an email with an **Accept invite** button. It takes them through a short signup flow — they create a password or sign in with Microsoft 365.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They appear in People">
    Refresh the People page — their status will flip from *Pending* to *Active* as soon as they accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The People page only invites full **admins**. If you need a read-only *member* role (someone who can view data but not change settings or billing), contact [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) — we can set that up for you.
</Note>

<Tip>
  **Inviting an assessor is a different flow.** Certified assessors who run in-person DSE visits are added by the Usafety team, not through the People page. If you need an assessor added to your account, email [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie).
</Tip>

## Resending or cancelling an invite

From **Settings → People**, click the menu next to any pending invite to resend or cancel it. Resending uses the original invite link — it won't expire the pending one.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="They didn't get the email">
    Ask them to check their spam folder first — invitation emails come from `no-reply@usafety.ie`. If it's not there, cancel the invite from the People page and send a fresh one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The invite link says it's expired">
    Invitation links expire after 14 days. Cancel the old invite and send a new one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="They used a different email when accepting">
    Usafety ties the invite to the email address you entered. If they sign up with a personal email instead, the invite won't bind. Cancel and re-invite with the correct address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I need to change someone from admin to read-only or remove them">
    Open **Settings → People**, find the person, and use the menu next to their name. To add a read-only member role, contact support — it's not self-service from the People page yet.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
