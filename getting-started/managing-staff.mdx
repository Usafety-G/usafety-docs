The **Staff Members** page is where every employee in your organisation lives. You'll spend more time here than anywhere else during an annual rollout or when chasing outstanding assessments.

## Opening the page

Click **Staff** in the sidebar. The page header reads *Staff Members* with a short subtitle — *View and manage staff members in your organization*.

## What's on the page

The staff table shows six columns by default:

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | Employee name and work email. Click the name to open their profile. |
| **Department** | Department assigned to the employee |
| **Assessment Status** | Their current assessment status badge |
| **Last Assessment** | When the most recent assessment was completed |
| **Assessor** | The assessor assigned to the most recent assessment (if any) |
| **Added** | When the employee was added to Usafety |

You can hide or show columns using the **Columns** selector in the toolbar.

## Adding staff

Two top-right buttons:

- **New Staff Member** — opens a dialog to add one employee by hand. Fill in name, email, department and any custom fields, then save.
- **Import / Export** — a dropdown with *Import CSV* (bulk upload from a spreadsheet) and *Export as CSV* (download the current filtered list).

For the CSV import flow in full, see [Importing staff via CSV](/importing-data/importing-staff).

## Searching and filtering

- **Search** — the search box at the top of the table matches on name and email
- **Advanced filter builder** — click the filter button to stack multiple conditions (e.g. *Department = Marketing AND Last Assessment > 12 months ago*). Useful for finding who's overdue or who hasn't been assessed yet.
- **Column sorting** — click any column header to sort ascending or descending

<Tip>
  A common filter combination for annual reassessment cycles: **Last Assessment date more than 12 months ago** → select all → use the CSV export to get a list of everyone who needs rebooking, then send them your current booking link.
</Tip>

## Bulk actions

Tick the checkbox on one or more rows to reveal the bulk action bar at the bottom of the page:

- **Export as CSV** — download the selected rows as a spreadsheet
- **Add Staff Member** — opens the add dialog (same as the top-right button)
- **Import CSV** — triggers the import flow

## The employee profile

Click any employee's name to open their profile page. It's split into three main tabs plus a right-hand sidebar.

### Main tabs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile">
    Their core details — name, email, department, job title, manager, and any custom fields. Editable if you're an admin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reports">
    Every assessment report this employee has ever had, with download links.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Assessments">
    The full history of assessments for this employee — scheduled, in progress, completed, cancelled — sortable by date.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Right sidebar

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Details">
    Quick-reference info — email, internal ID, date they joined Usafety.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Activity">
    An audit log of every action taken on this employee record. Useful for compliance questions ("when did we send them the booking link?", "when did they complete their last assessment?").
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Comments">
    A comment thread attached to the employee. Use it for HR notes, accommodation context, or reminders for your team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
