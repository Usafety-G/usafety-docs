Usafety sends a fixed set of transactional emails automatically and exposes a customisable **weekly compliance summary** in your settings. This page covers both.

## Automatic emails

These can't currently be turned off or reconfigured — they're part of the core flow.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="To employees" icon="user">
    - **Booking confirmation** — when they book a slot on a public booking link
    - **Booking reminder** — a reminder before their scheduled assessment
    - **Teams meeting link** — for remote bookings, the Microsoft Teams join URL
    - **Reschedule notification** — when an admin reschedules their booking
    - **Cancellation notification** — when an admin cancels their booking
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="To organisation admins" icon="shield-halved">
    - **Invitation** — when someone invites you
    - **Booking link approved** — when the Usafety team approves a booking link you created
    - **Booking link rejected** — with the reason, so you can fix and resubmit
    - **New booking created** — when an employee books via one of your links
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="To assessors" icon="user-doctor">
    - **New booking assigned** — when an employee books a slot on a link assigned to them
    - **Reschedule / cancellation** — when an admin changes a booking
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## The weekly summary email

Usafety can email a weekly (or fortnightly / monthly) compliance summary to one or more recipients. It's configured from **Settings → Reports & Emails**.

See [Reports & emails](/for-administrators/reports-and-emails) for the full guide to turning it on, choosing recipients and setting the frequency.

## What you can't configure yet

- Turning specific transactional notifications on or off (booking confirmations, reminders, etc.)
- Changing the frequency of reminders
- Adding additional recipients to transactional emails (e.g. CC'ing a manager)
- Custom email branding beyond what's in Reports & Emails

If any of these are blocking, let us know at [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie).
