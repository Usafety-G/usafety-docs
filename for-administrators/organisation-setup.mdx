This is a checklist for a new admin starting a fresh Usafety organisation. Most of it is covered in more detail on the [Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart) — this page is the 60-second version.

## Week-one checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept your invite">
    Open the email from Usafety, click **Accept invite**, create a password or sign in with Microsoft 365.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your backup admins">
    **Settings → People → Invite Admin**. Add at least one backup so you're not a single point of failure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build out the equipment catalogue">
    **Settings → Equipment → Add equipment**. Seed it with the three or four items your assessors most often recommend — chairs, monitor arms, footrests, keyboards. You can add more later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first booking link">
    **Booking Links → Create Link**. Pick the dates, times and type (remote or in-person). It'll go into Pending for Usafety approval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the approved link with employees">
    Once the status flips to **Approved**, copy the public URL and share it via email, Slack, Teams or your intranet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch bookings come in">
    Each booking appears in the **Calendar** view and the **Equipment Compliance** report as the assessments complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Usafety does *not* do at setup time

There's no interactive onboarding wizard or first-run setup flow. You land on the dashboard after accepting your invite and take it from there. If you get stuck, email [support@usafety.ie](mailto:support@usafety.ie) — we can walk you through it on a call.
