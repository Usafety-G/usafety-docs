Usafety has a dedicated **Reports & Emails** settings page where you can personalise the report each employee receives after their assessment and turn on a recurring compliance summary email for your team.

## Opening the page

Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Reports & Emails**. The page header reads *Reports & Emails*.

## Customising the employee assessment report

When an employee finishes their assessment, Usafety emails them their report automatically. You can add your organisation's touch to that email:

- **Custom message** — a short paragraph added near the top of the email, e.g. *"If you have any questions about your results, please contact H&S at wellbeing@example.com."*
- **Custom link** — an optional URL you'd like the employee to click (a wellness portal, an HR form, a booking link for a follow-up)
- **Custom link label** — the text shown on the button for the custom link

All three are optional. If you don't set them, employees get Usafety's default email.

## The weekly compliance summary email

Usafety can send a recurring compliance summary to your admins or SLT so no one has to log in to check the numbers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the summary">
    Tick the enable toggle on **Settings → Reports & Emails**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose recipients">
    Add one or more email addresses. This is where the summary gets sent — often a H&S team alias plus one SLT address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the frequency">
    Choose *Weekly*, *Fortnightly* or *Monthly*. Weekly is the default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The summary starts sending from the next scheduled run.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What's in the summary email

The summary covers the same core numbers you'd see on the organisation dashboard:

- How many assessments were booked, completed and cancelled in the period
- Current compliance percentage
- Items that moved into *Needs Attention* since the last summary
- A quick glance at the Recent Booking Links list

If you want anything else added to the summary, email [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) — we're actively iterating on what's in it.

## Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use the custom message for signposting, not support">
    The employee assessment email goes to dozens or hundreds of people at once. Don't put a specific person's name or calendar link in it — use a team alias or a shared inbox.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stagger the weekly summary with your team's Monday review">
    Schedule the summary to arrive on a Monday morning so it's in your inbox when you sit down to triage the week's H&S work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Add SLT as a monthly recipient, H&S as weekly">
    The settings page currently supports one frequency. If you need two cadences (weekly to H&S, monthly to SLT), email support and we can configure both on the back end.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
