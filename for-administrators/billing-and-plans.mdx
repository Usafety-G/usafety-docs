Usafety is moving toward self-service billing. Today, billing is handled by the Usafety team directly rather than through an in-app checkout.

## What you'll see in-app

Go to **Settings → Plans** to see the three tiers Usafety offers:

- **Starter**
- **Professional**
- **Enterprise**

Each plan card shows the features it includes. The page currently displays a *"Plan upgrades coming soon"* message — that's the self-service upgrade flow still being built.

## How to change your plan today

Until self-service is live, contact [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) to:

- **Upgrade or downgrade** your plan
- **Get a quote** for Enterprise or custom pricing
- **Change billing details** (VAT number, billing email, purchase order requirements)
- **Request a past invoice**
- **Set up annual billing** instead of monthly

We reply during Irish business hours and typically process changes the same day.

<Note>
  **Self-service billing is on the roadmap.** When it ships, you'll be able to upgrade, change payment method, and download invoices from this page directly.
</Note>
