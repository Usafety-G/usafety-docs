The **Workspace** settings page is where you edit the basic identity of your Usafety organisation — the name that appears in invitations and reports, the contact details you want employees to see, and the email domains that are allowed to register or book.

## Opening the page

Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Workspace**.

## What you can edit today

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Organisation name" icon="building">
    The name shown on invitation emails, assessment reports and the header of public booking pages. This is usually your legal trading name (e.g. *Acme Manufacturing Ireland Ltd*) rather than a shortened version.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Allowed email domains" icon="at">
    A list of email domains that are allowed to join your organisation or use your booking links. For example: `acme.ie` and `acme.co.uk`. Anyone trying to book with an email outside these domains will get rejected.

    Leave empty to allow any email address (not recommended — use this only if you genuinely need to let external contractors book).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Fields you'll see but can't edit yet

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Website" icon="globe">
    Your organisation's public website. This field is visible on the page but currently locked — it'll be saved in a future update. Until then, contact [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) if you need your website set or changed on the record.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Phone" icon="phone">
    A contact number. Also visible but currently locked for the same reason. Contact support to set it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why allowed domains matter

The allowed-domains list is your main defence against random people finding a booking link on the internet and taking slots meant for your staff. Keep it tight:

- **Include every domain your staff actually use** — e.g. a pre-acquisition legacy domain
- **Exclude personal providers** — `gmail.com`, `outlook.com`, `yahoo.com`, etc.
- **Review after any rebrand or acquisition** — adding the new domain and retiring the old one

If an employee reports that they can't book because their email is rejected, check this list first.

## Editing and saving

Edit any field, then click **Save** at the bottom of the page. Changes take effect immediately and don't require anyone to log out.
