Usafety has two Microsoft integrations. One is set-and-forget, the other is automatic.

## Sign in with Microsoft (SSO)

Any Usafety user can sign in with their Microsoft 365 account instead of a password. On the login screen, click **Sign in with Microsoft** and you'll be taken through the standard Azure AD consent flow. Once you've granted consent the first time, subsequent sign-ins are one click.

SSO is available platform-wide — there's nothing to configure in your organisation settings, and it works for any Microsoft 365 tenant.

<Note>
  Microsoft SSO is per-user, not per-organisation. If your organisation requires SSO to be mandatory (i.e. users cannot sign in with a password), contact [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) — we can enforce that on your account.
</Note>

## Teams meetings for remote assessments

When an assessor runs a remote DSE assessment, Usafety automatically generates a Microsoft Teams meeting link and includes it in the booking confirmation email. Employees click the link in their email at the scheduled time to join the call — no Teams account required on their side.

This happens automatically for any booking link with **Booking type: Remote**. There's nothing to enable.

## What Usafety does *not* sync

- **Outlook calendar sync** — Usafety does not push bookings into your Outlook calendar. Bookings live in the Usafety calendar only.
- **Email sync** — Usafety does not read or send from your Outlook mailbox.
- **Org-wide directory sync** — Usafety does not import your Microsoft 365 user directory automatically. Staff are added via [CSV import](/importing-data/importing-staff) or manually.

If any of these would be useful for your team, let us know at [info@usafety.ie](mailto:info@usafety.ie) — it helps prioritise the roadmap.
