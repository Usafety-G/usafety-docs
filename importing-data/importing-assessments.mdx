---
title: "Importing assessment history"
description: "Bring existing DSE assessment records into Usafety from another system."
---

<Note>
  This page is being written. Usafety supports importing historical assessments via CSV — the flow is the same as [Importing staff](/importing-data/importing-staff), but you'll upload to **Assessments → Import** instead.

  If you're migrating from another platform and need help mapping fields, email [support@usafety.ie](mailto:support@usafety.ie) — we've helped several organisations with this.
</Note>
