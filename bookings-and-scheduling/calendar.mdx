title: "Managing the calendar"
description: "See every booked assessment across your organisation and jump into any one of them."
---

The Calendar view shows every assessment booked through your booking links, colour-coded by status and filterable by assessor or booking link.

## Opening the calendar

Click **Calendar** in the organisation sidebar. You'll see a month view by default; switch to week or day using the toggle in the top-right.

## What's on the calendar

Each event on the calendar is a booked assessment. Clicking it opens a side panel with:

- **Employee name and email**
- **Booking link** the employee came in from
- **Time slot** (morning or afternoon)
- **Booking type** (remote or in-person)
- **Location** (for in-person) with address and Eircode
- **Status** — scheduled, in progress, completed, cancelled
- Any **custom field values** the employee filled in when booking

## Filtering

Use the filter controls at the top of the page to narrow to:

- A specific **booking link** (useful if you're running multiple rollouts at once)
- A specific **assessor**
- A specific **date range**

## Rescheduling from the calendar

Click any booking in the calendar to open the side panel, then click **Reschedule** to move it. The employee and assessor both get an email when the time changes. Full guide: [Rescheduling & cancellations](/bookings-and-scheduling/rescheduling).
