---
title: "Rescheduling & cancellations"
description: "Move or cancel an employee's booked DSE assessment."
---

Employees occasionally need to change the time they picked, or an assessor has to cancel at short notice. Usafety handles both from the Calendar view.

## Reschedule a booking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Calendar">
    Click **Calendar** in the organisation sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the booking">
    Navigate to the date the booking is currently on. Click the event to open the side panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Reschedule">
    Pick a new date and time from the available slots on the same booking link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Both the employee and the assessor receive an email with the updated time. The booking moves in the calendar immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Rescheduled bookings stay linked to their original booking link, so they still count against the booking link's capacity and date range.
</Tip>

## Cancel a booking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the booking from the Calendar">
    Same as above — click the event to open the side panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    You'll be asked to confirm. Optionally add a reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation emails go out">
    The employee and assessor both receive a cancellation email. The booking is removed from the calendar but kept in the Assessments list with a *cancelled* status for audit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Can an employee reschedule themselves?

Not yet. If an employee needs to change their slot, they should contact you (the admin) and you reschedule on their behalf from the Calendar. This is on the roadmap.
