---
title: "Sending an assessment"
description: "Send a DSE self-assessment to one employee or in bulk."
---

## Send to one employee

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the employee">
    Go to **Staff** in the sidebar and click the employee's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send assessment">
    On the employee profile, click **Send assessment** in the top-right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the type">
    Pick **DSE self-assessment** (or whichever template your organisation has configured). If you're unsure, self-assessment is the default for annual compliance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send">
    The employee gets an email immediately with a link that's unique to them. You'll see the assessment show up under the employee's **Assessments** tab as *Sent*.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send to many employees at once

If you're running an annual compliance cycle and need to send assessments to a whole team:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Staff">
    Click **Staff** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter and select">
    Filter by department or any custom field, then tick the checkboxes for the employees you want — or tick the header checkbox to select everyone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send assessments">
    The bulk action bar at the bottom of the page will show a **Send assessments** button. Click it, pick the assessment type, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track progress">
    Open **Assessments** in the sidebar to see every assessment in *Sent* state. As employees complete them, they flip to *Completed*.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reminder emails

Usafety automatically sends reminders to employees who haven't completed their assessment after a few days. You can also trigger a manual reminder from the assessment row — click the row and then **Resend invite**.
