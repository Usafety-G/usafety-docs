---
title: "Remediation workflow"
description: "Track your team's workflow on acting on assessment findings."
---

Remediation status is how your team tracks what it's actually doing about assessment findings. It runs alongside the automatic compliance status (which Usafety derives from the data) so you can see both *where an employee stands* and *where your team is on actioning it*.

## The four states

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **No action needed** | The assessment is fine. No follow-up required. |
| **To do** | You've reviewed the assessment, there's work to do, but you haven't started yet. |
| **In progress** | Actively working on it — chasing suppliers, awaiting SLT approval, scheduling an install. |
| **Done** | Everything's been ordered, installed, and confirmed with the employee. |

## Setting the status

Remediation status can be changed from two places:

1. **The assessment Quick View** — open any row on the **Assessments** page and pick from the dropdown.
2. **The compliance dashboard** — on **Reports → Equipment Compliance**, inline-edit the status column.

## Why it exists

Usafety's automatic compliance status only knows about the data — it'll flip to *compliant* as soon as all recommended equipment is marked *completed*. But that moment might be weeks away; in the meantime, your SLT wants to know *what you're doing about it*.

Remediation status fills that gap. It's your workflow, not the system's judgement.

## Filtering by remediation status

On the **Equipment Compliance** dashboard, filter by remediation status to answer specific questions:

- **To do** — my backlog
- **In progress** — things I should chase this week
- **Done** — items to spot-check in the next reassessment
