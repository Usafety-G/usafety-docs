DSE assessments aren't one-and-done. Workstations change, people's health changes, and Irish H&S guidance recommends regular reviews.

## When to re-assess

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Annually">
    The most common cadence. Run an annual cycle in the same month every year so employees know what to expect.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When the workstation changes">
    New desk, new chair, new monitor, office move, shift from office to home-working. Any of these should trigger a re-assessment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="After a health change">
    Back injury, pregnancy, any new accommodation request — re-assess to make sure the workstation still suits the employee.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="After a complaint or incident">
    If an employee reports discomfort, tingling or headaches linked to their workstation, re-assess immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Running an annual cycle

The easiest way to run an annual re-assessment cycle is to create a new booking link with this year's dates and share it with the employees who need re-assessing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new booking link">
    Go to **Booking Links** and click **Create Link**. Use a name like *2026 annual re-assessment* so it's easy to tell apart from ad-hoc bookings. See the full guide at [Booking links](/bookings-and-scheduling/booking-links).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    Usafety approves new links the same day during Irish business hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Staff by last assessment date">
    On the **Staff** page, filter by *Last assessment date = more than 12 months ago* — those are the employees who need re-assessing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the booking link with them">
    Email, Slack, Teams, or your intranet — whatever works. Employees click it, pick a slot, and book themselves.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor completion">
    Track progress on the **Assessments** page. New completions don't overwrite old assessments — the history stays on the employee profile for audit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you'd rather schedule the whole team yourself instead of letting them choose, use [Importing staff via CSV](/importing-data/importing-staff) with a pre-set date and assessor.
</Tip>
