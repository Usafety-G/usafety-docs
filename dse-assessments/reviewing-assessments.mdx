---
title: "Reviewing a completed assessment"
description: "Triage a submitted DSE assessment, set remediation status, and push recommendations into the compliance dashboard."
---

Once an employee submits their assessment, you'll get an email and it shows up as *Completed* on the **Assessments** page. This is where you decide whether they need equipment, follow-up or nothing at all.

## Open the assessment

Go to **Assessments** in the sidebar. The list sorts completed rows to the top. Click a row to open the Quick View side panel.

## What you'll see

- **Employee details** — name, department, email
- **Answers to each question** — chair, desk, monitor, keyboard, posture, lighting
- **Any photos** the employee uploaded
- **Auto-generated recommendations** — equipment or adjustments Usafety suggested based on the answers
- **Risk level** — low, moderate or high, based on the scoring

## Triage the recommendations

For each recommended item:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Accept it">
    Leave it as-is. It'll appear on the Equipment Compliance dashboard as *Pending* fulfilment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Modify it">
    Click the item to change the model, quantity or notes before accepting. Useful if your organisation uses a specific chair model you want to recommend instead of the generic one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reject it">
    If the recommendation doesn't apply (e.g. the employee already has that equipment at home and the assessment misread the question), reject it with a note.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Set the remediation status

The remediation status dropdown is how *your* team tracks its workflow. Options:

- **No action needed** — the assessment is fine, nothing to do
- **To do** — acknowledged, you'll act soon
- **In progress** — actively ordering or fitting equipment
- **Done** — everything's sorted

This status is independent of the automatic **compliance status**, which Usafety derives from whether the employee still has outstanding equipment needs. See [Compliance dashboard](/equipment-and-compliance/compliance-dashboard) for the difference.

## Add a comment

Every assessment has a comment thread. Use it for:

- Phone-call notes ("called to check on chair delivery")
- PO numbers
- Escalations to SLT
- Reasons for rejecting a recommendation

Comments appear on the exported PDF and form part of your audit trail.

## Download the report

Click **Download Report** in the Quick View to get a PDF of this single assessment. Useful for HR files or one-off SLT approvals. For bulk export, see [Exporting equipment reports](/equipment-and-compliance/exporting-equipment-reports).
