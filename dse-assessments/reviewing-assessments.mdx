---
title: "Reviewing a completed assessment"
description: "Open the Quick View for a completed DSE assessment, triage the equipment recommendations and download a report."
---

Once an employee's assessment is complete, it shows up on the **Reports → Equipment Compliance** page. The Quick View side panel is where you do the day-to-day work of reviewing it.

## Open the Quick View

Go to **Reports → Equipment Compliance** in the sidebar. Find the row for the employee — filter by name, department or assessment date if needed — then click the Quick View button on the right of the row. A side panel opens on the right.

## What's in the Quick View

The side panel contains five sections:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Employee Details" icon="user">
    Name, email, department, work location, the date of the most recent assessment and the name of the assessor who completed it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compliance Status" icon="circle-check">
    The overall compliance status (*Compliant*, *Non-compliant* or *Not assessed*) and the current remediation status badge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Equipment Required" icon="box">
    Every piece of equipment the assessor recommended, with its current fulfilment state (Recommended, Approved, Ordered, Delivered, Installed, Completed, Rejected, Returned). Each row has an inline dropdown so you can change the state as you act on it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Organization Notes" icon="note-sticky">
    Any notes attached to the employee or the assessment by you or the assessor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Comments / Activity" icon="comments">
    A thread of comments attached to this assessment. Use it for PO numbers, supplier calls, SLT approvals or the reason a recommendation was declined.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Actions menu

Click the **⋮** menu in the Quick View header for:

- **Download Report** — exports a single PDF of this assessment
- **Add Comment** — opens a sheet to add to the comment thread

## Updating equipment items

Each item in the *Equipment Required* list has a status dropdown. Changing it updates the fulfilment lifecycle immediately. The available states are:

*Recommended, Approved, Ordered, Delivered, Installed, Completed, Rejected, Returned*

See [Fulfilment workflow](/equipment-and-compliance/fulfilment-workflow) for what each state means.

## Setting remediation status

Remediation status is how your team tracks its own workflow on acting on the findings. It's edited inline from the Equipment Compliance **table** (not from the Quick View — the Quick View shows it as a read-only badge).

Options: *No Action Needed*, *To Do*, *In Progress*, *Done*. See [Remediation workflow](/dse-assessments/remediation-workflow) for the full detail.

## Downloading the report

Click **Download Report** from the Quick View menu to get a PDF of this single assessment. It contains employee details, compliance status, every recommended equipment item with cost and supplier, and any comments. Useful for HR files, one-off SLT approvals or audit requests.

For bulk export of many assessments at once, see [Exporting equipment reports](/equipment-and-compliance/exporting-equipment-reports).
